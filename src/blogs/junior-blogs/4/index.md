## another project?

mr. christy told me one day that we have a bunch of kb2040's, and we needed to do something with them.

i decided to take a look into it, as he mentioned that it's for keyboards, which is something i'm quite passionate about.

![kb2040](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/106/893/large1024/adafruit_products_KeeBoar_top.jpg?1638290681)

it's main use case is for custom keyboards, such as macro pads or numpads and stuff like that. however, i wanna make something bigger if i do decide to use it.

i have a very ambitious plan of creating an entire custom keyboard from scratch (maybe not the entire thing as the case would be hard to get), but the pcb would have to be custom to use the kb2040.
