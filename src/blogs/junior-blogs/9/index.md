# junior week oct. 30th, 2025

## ap lang project

in ap lang, we got a project in which we have to identify rhetorical devices in poems, as well as bring the poem to life.

my poem was harlem, also known as "dream deferred" by langston hughes.

![the poem](/static/img/junior/9/harlem.webp)
###### the poem. i suggest reading it if you wanna understand the other parts.

## the idea

so my original idea was to create the word "dream" while having each of the letters represent each one of the vivid imagery that he uses.

this is what it ended up looking like:

![draft idea](/static/img/junior/9/draft.webp)
###### "d" would be covered in raisins, "r" would be an open sore, "e" would be the colors of rotten meat, "a" would be crystallized with honey, and "m" would be compressed under a weight.

however, i didn't really like the idea because i couldn't figure out a way to incorporate the last part, the explosion.

i talked with some friends about capacitor explosions, but ended up not doing it to preserve resources.

in the end, i decided to just restart, focusing on the "or does it explode" line the most, as i felt that it was the most important aspect of the poem.
